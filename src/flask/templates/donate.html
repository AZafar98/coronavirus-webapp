FareShare

FareShare is the UK’s national network of charitable food redistributors, made up of 17 independent organisations. Together, we take good quality surplus food from right across the food industry and get it to almost 11,000 frontline charities and community groups.

During this crisis, it is more important than ever to support vulnerable people. Help FareShare to deliver more food to those most in need.

Meals for the NHS

Help buy meals for the NHS staff fighting COVID-19 After 18-hour shifts trying to save our lives from COVID-19, NHS Hospital Staff are faced with canteens that shut at 5pm .

Refuge

On any given day Refuge supports more than 6,000 clients, helping them rebuild their lives and overcome many different forms of violence and abuse; for example domestic violence, sexual violence, so-called ‘honour’-based violence, human trafficking and modern slavery, and female genital mutilation.

With domestic violence incidents increasing during the lockdown, it is important now more than ever to support Refuge.
